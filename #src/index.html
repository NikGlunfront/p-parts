About us
Who we are

We are a young agile startup with a modern approach to supply the printing industry with used press parts worldwide. Our office and warehouse are located in Vienna, Austria. We want to become the leading supplier for used equipment parts.

Where are we going

We are working hard to enlarge our assortment with other brand parts in the near future.

What we do

Most of our used press parts are shown in three different conditions: almost as new, very good and used. Most stock items are used machine parts, test parts, dismantled parts from old machines and new sell out parts bought from manufacturers.

Our service

We are here to help, support and maintain our customers through fair pricing & prompt delivery. With our logistic partners, we are able to ship used replacement parts to over 200 countries worldwide.

What to expect

With a high value inventory, most parts ship the same day. Our stock consists of more than 60.000 replacement parts for Heidelberg machines, including a decent collection of 'hard-to-find' parts. Through our own stock and our vast network, we can offer parts that you may not be able to find elsewhere.

Please be aware that many products are limited stocks.
